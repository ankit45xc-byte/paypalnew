Troubleshooting Guides

Can’t Sign In with Apple ID

Try resetting your password at iforgot.apple.com. If still locked out, call support for account recovery help.

iPhone Not Charging

Check your cable, clean the port, and restart the device. If unresolved, schedule a repair via AppleCare.

Mac Running Slow

Check storage, update macOS, and close unused apps. Support can guide you through optimization.

Beware of phishing emails claiming to be Apple. Apple will never ask for your Apple ID password or verification codes via email or SMS.
